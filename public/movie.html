Top Movies of All Time According to Deema

My best 3 movies.

Inception

A thief who steals corporate secrets through the use of dream-sharing technology is given the inverse task of planting an idea into the mind of a CEO.

Interstellar

The film follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for mankind.

Beauty and the Beast

Beauty and the Beast is a 2017 American musical romantic fantasy film directed by Bill Condon from a screenplay by Stephen Chbosky and Evan Spiliotopoulos.
